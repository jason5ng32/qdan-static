美剧电视剧

2015年春季档美剧推荐

前面是比较期待新剧首播，后面是我在追的剧集回归，纯粹个人喜好啦。

排名不分先后，首字母排序。

  1. 12 Monkeys 十二猴子（Syfy：2015年1月16日）

    烧脑科幻剧！Syfy翻拍经典电影，不容错过。男主是Nikita里的小Geek。

    12 Monkeys (TV Series 2015– )
    Created by Travis Fickett, Terry Matalas. With Aaron Stanford, Amanda Schull, Noah Bean, Barbara Sukowa. Follows the journey of a time traveler from the post-apocalyptic future who appears in present day on a mission to locate and eradicate the source of a deadly plague that will nearly destroy the human race.
      imdb.com

    • CSI: Cyber 犯罪现场调查：网络（CBS：2015年3月5日）

      CSI已经很geek了，网络犯罪期待值更加。

      CSI: Cyber (TV Series 2015– )
      Created by Ann Donahue, Carol Mendelsohn, Anthony E. Zuiker. With Patricia Arquette, James Van Der Beek, Peter MacNicol, Shad Moss. Special agent Avery Ryan works to solve crimes as a CyberPsychologist for the FBI.
        imdb.com

      • Allegiance 忠诚（NBC：2015年2月5日）

        俄罗斯间谍/特工家庭在美帝~节奏快，男主geek萌，故事线复杂，推荐！

      • Fresh Off the Boat 初来乍到（ABC：2015年2月4日）

        这部单镜头情景喜剧根据华裔美国厨师黄颐铭（Eddie Huang）的回忆录《初来乍到》改编，主要描述上世纪九十年代一个台湾家庭来到奥兰多定居的故事。

        美剧里讲中国家庭融入的喜剧比较少，拉丁裔偏多。这部挺好的，可以一看！

      • Backstrom 丧心病探（FOX ：2015年1月22日）

        侦探版豪斯医生不得不期待一下。

        Backstrom (TV Series 2015– )
        Created by Hart Hanson. With Rainn Wilson, Genevieve Angelson, Dennis Haysbert, Page Kennedy. Backstrom, the renowned misanthropic police detective, takes on new and daunting cases.
          imdb.com

        • Battle Creek 错搭警员（CBS ：2015年3月2日）

          双男主设定+罪案剧，基情满满不解释。

          Battle Creek (TV Series 2015– )
          Created by Vince Gilligan, David Shore. With Josh Duhamel, Dean Winters, Aubrey Dollar, Grapevine. Two detectives with different views on the world team up and using cynicism, guile and deception, they clean up the streets of Battle Creek.
            imdb.com

          • Eye Candy 糖心陷阱（MTV ：2015年1月12日）（已弃）

            有关黑客的肥皂剧~

            Eye Candy (TV Series 2015– )
            Created by Christian Taylor. With Victoria Justice, Casey Jon Deidrick, Harvey Guillen, Marcus Callender. A New York woman suspects that one of her online dates is a serial killer.
              imdb.com

            • Marvels Agent Carter 卡特特工（ABC：2015年1月6日）

              咱接着美国队长继续唠哈~

              Agent Carter (TV Series 2015– )
              Created by Christopher Markus, Stephen McFeely. With Hayley Atwell, James D'Arcy, Chad Michael Murray, Enver Gjokaj. In 1946, Peggy Carter is relegated to secretarial duties in the Strategic Scientific Reserve (SSR). When Howard Stark is accused of treason, he secretly recruits Peggy to clear his name with the help of his butler, Edwin Jarvis.
                imdb.com

              • The Last Man On Earth 地球上最后一个人（FOX ：2015年3月1日）

                我只想知道，只有一个主演的单镜头喜剧会另类成什么样子……

                The Last Man on Earth (TV Series 2015– )
                Created by Will Forte. With Will Forte, January Jones, Cleopatra Coleman, Kristen Schaal. THE LAST MAN ON EARTH is a new single-camera comedy about the life and adventures of the last man on earth. PHIL MILLER (Forte) was once just an average guy who loved his family and hated his job at the bank - now he's humanity's last hope. Will he ever find another person alive on the planet? Would hoping that she is a female be asking too much?
                  imdb.com

                • The Librarians 图书管理员（TNT：2014年12月7日）

                  看似玄乎实则轻松的魔法剧。

                  The Librarians (TV Series 2014– )
                  Created by John Rogers. With Rebecca Romijn, Christian Kane, Lindy Booth, John Kim. A group of librarians set off on adventures in an effort to save mysterious, ancient artifacts.
                    imdb.com
